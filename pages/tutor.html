Personalized tutoring for STEM competitions

Preparation for topics and questions encountered in the typical pre-collegiate and collegiate competition math track, including but not limited to: algebra, probability, combinatorics, geometry, trigonometry, and number theory. Preparation for general science competitions, including Science Olympiad, National Science Bowl, USABO, USNCO, or USACO, is offered but may be subject to different rates. If you are interested, please contact for specifics.

I work with students of all backgrounds and ages. I firmly believe that it's never too early and never too late to get started.

At the beginning of each month, I devise a unique curriculum which takes into account the student's performance and long-term goals. A copy of this syllabus is sent to the student and the parent. Each week, I meet with the student once or twice. Each session is either one or two hours.

For more information, inquiries and messages can be directed to me via email (d4vidwang@gmail.com).
